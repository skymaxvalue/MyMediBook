Making the most of your visit

Care providers often manage complex schedules to ensure every patient receives the attention they need. To maximize your time with your doctor and ensure a relaxed experience, we recommend the following steps:

Arrive early, stay relaxed

Plan to arrive at the office 10 to 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment. This allows ample time for:

  1. Complete any necessary check-in paperwork.
  2. Record your vital signs (such as height, weight, and blood pressure).
  3. Ensure you are ready to see the doctor the moment they become available.

Stay organized

To help keep your day running smoothly, we suggest recording your appointment date and time in a personal calendar or digital planner as soon as it is scheduled. Incorporating your visit into your daily routine helps prevent any last-minute rushes and ensures you feel prepared for your consultation.
